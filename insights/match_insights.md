# Match Analytics Insights

## Most Played Maps

Mirage represents the majority of matches played on the platform, indicating strong player preference for traditional competitive maps.

## Low Adoption Maps

Cache and Overpass show extremely low pick rates, potentially indicating:
- low community interest
- matchmaking imbalance
- map pool preference concentration

## Match Completion

Approximately 90% of matches are completed successfully.

Around 10% of matches are cancelled, requiring further investigation into queue flow and acceptance process.
